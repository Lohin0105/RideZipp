About RideZipp

RideZipp is a carpooling platform designed to help you share rides, save money, and reduce your carbon footprint. Our mission is to make transportation more efficient, affordable, and eco-friendly.

Features:

  • Easy ride creation and booking.
  • Real-time ride tracking.
  • Secure payment options.
  • 24/7 customer support.
